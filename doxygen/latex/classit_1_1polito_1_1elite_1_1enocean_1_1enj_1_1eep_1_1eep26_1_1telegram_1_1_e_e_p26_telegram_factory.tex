\subsection{Detailed Description}
A factory class for genetaring proper \hyperlink{classit_1_1polito_1_1elite_1_1enocean_1_1enj_1_1eep_1_1eep26_1_1telegram_1_1_e_e_p26_telegram}{E\+E\+P26\+Telegram} instances given a low level \hyperlink{}{E\+S\+P3\+Packet} containing a telegram as payload. While it currently works only on the fixed set of telegrams defined in the e\+E\+P2.\+6 specification, it will be extended to automatically include newly added telegram types.
