\subsection{Detailed Description}
A class representing the energy measurement function associated to some of the E\+E\+Ps defined in the 2.\+6 specification. Energy values could either be in Wh or in k\+Wh and they are treated as a couple Number-\/unit with no explicit unit of measurement handling.
