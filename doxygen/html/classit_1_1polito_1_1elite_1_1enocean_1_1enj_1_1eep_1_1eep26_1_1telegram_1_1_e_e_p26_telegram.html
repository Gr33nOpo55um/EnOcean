Detailed Description

This class epresents a data telegram transferred between two EnOcean devices, one of which is typically a gateway. According to the EnOcean Equipment Profile specification, telegrams can be of 5 different types: 1BS, 4BS, RBS, UTE and VLD. For each type a subclass of this EEP26Telegram abstract class is defined.
